Hello! My name is Kaity Goodwin and I want to be your 2021-2022 Campus Activities Board President!

My CAB History

I have been involved with CAB since I was a freshman. I attended almost all the Welcome Week activities, and some of you may even recognize me from my infamous Bingo wni (winning a TV and Delta Vouchers in the same game. After my high school event planning experience and my passion for the CAB events I had attended thus far, I knew I wanted to be a part of CAB and so I applied for the events committee. Through my experience on the events committee, I learned the ins and outs of large scale events at Babson. I got a hand in planning the Winter Welcome Week events, got to design several t shirts and giveaways, and even created all of the Offset Spring Concert marketing materials!
Sophomore year, I applied for and was elected as co VP of Events. Here, I planned over 60 events for the Babson community including several homegrown events such as the welcome back photo scavenger hunt, music bingo, and Cash CAB. I approached every event with passion, creativity, and dedication. I also loved ordering all the prizes and giveaways for fan favorite events such as trivia and Bingo.
When it came to the school shutting down in March, I played an integral part in helping our team pivot to online events and working closely with my Co-VP of Events in turning the rest of our semester into virtual events for everyone. We created an efficient online Bingo system, ran fun and new photo scavenger hunts and challenges, and I was charged with the difficult task of distributing and managing prizes for all of our off campus winners.
Junior year, I pivoted from events and assumed the position of co-VP of Marketing. Here, I streamlined and created an efficient and effective CAB brand for all of our instagram and social media marketing. I knew I wanted CAB to have a cohesive brand so that everything was quickly and well recognizable. We also focused heavily on digital marketing in our new virtual world, as well as thinking of new in-person marketing strategies such as our pop up booths and the upcoming events board in Reynolds behind the info desk. I loved ordering all the CAB Giveaways for the pop up booths and bringing fun and useful items to the Babson community!

My Goals as CAB President

In this time, we never know what to expect even the next day. Things are constantly changing and the state of our country and of our community is also ever changing. So far this year, I have loved supporting in-person events and bringing students that joy of CAB events that we are so familiar with. As president, I would strive to ensure that we are able to still have in-person events and do my best to create a safe environment for these things to happen. Given my creative nature and background and strong event planning history, I am confident that I will be able to continue to think outside the box and provide everyone with more engaging and in-person activities.
